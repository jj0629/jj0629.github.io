Lost & Found: New Harvest

Lost & Found is an educational card game series meant to teach players about religious laws pertaining to lost-and-found articles in medieval Cairo. This title is the first stand-alone expansion of the series! Players must work together to solve the community's needs. At the same time, they must help themselves and their families in an attempt to keep from falling into poverty and hunger. This game does its best to be historically accurate to the religious laws while, at the same time, being engaging and fun to play!

More information on the game and the rest of the series can be found at the game's website here. In addition, you can purchase Lost & Found: New Harvest on The Game Crafter.

My work for this game included historical research of medieval religious laws to keep the game period accurate. I designed event cards and game mechanics. In addition, I playtested and QA tested before release.
